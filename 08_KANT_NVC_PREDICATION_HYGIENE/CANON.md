## Current Provisional Core

Human language often gives the same grammatical predication shape to different kinds of judgment:

```text
X is red
X is beautiful
X is useful
X is irresponsible
X does not respect me
```

The draft claim is that violent or conflict-escalating communication often begins when a subject's relation, affect, need, or interpretation is grammatically projected as an objective property of the other person or object.

Nonviolent Communication can therefore be interpreted as a practical discipline of predication hygiene:

```text
violent communication
= subjective relation masked as an objective property of the other

nonviolent communication
= subjective relation returned to the subject and connected to a clear request
```

This provisional core is hypothesis-level only.
