The current material is a non-canon research program: reconstructing the
development of human thought from the total record of what humans have created,
left, stabilized, transmitted, and transformed.

Use `HYPOTHESES/`, `SOURCES/`, `CASES/`, and `PATCHES/` until the program has:

- source-backed scope boundaries;
- explicit method rules;
- test cases across multiple artifact classes;
- a bias and falsification map;
- review against R Theory, Culture as Instructions, MEAT, CID, and archaeology
  source constraints.

Do not treat artifact form as direct access to mental states.
