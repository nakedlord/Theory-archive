## Purpose

This module stores the user's broad research program:

```text
look at everything humans have ever created and use that total artifact record
to reconstruct the development of human thought
```

It is broader than:

- R Theory archaeology;
- action-first abstraction;
- Culture as Externalized Instructions;
- cultural drift;
- MEAT;
- CID.

Those theories become lenses inside this research program.

## Core Distinction

The target is not a general history of culture.

The target is:

```text
history of changing ways of thinking, inferred through the traces humans made
and transmitted
```

Artifacts are used broadly:

- stone tools;
- fire traces and camps;
- burials and body treatment;
- ornaments and images;
- architecture;
- music and rhythm;
- myth, ritual, religion, law;
- mathematics, philosophy, science;
- institutions;
- writing and media;
- machines, software, interfaces, and AI systems.
