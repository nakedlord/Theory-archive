## Working Name

Human Thought Reconstruction from the Total Artifact Record.

Short internal names:

- `Human Thought Reconstruction`;
- `Total Artifact Record`;
- `Artifact Record of Thought`.

## Working Question

```text
If we look at everything humans have ever created, what can we reconstruct
about the development of human thought?
```

## Working Formula

```text
human-created trace
-> operation or distinction stabilized in matter, body, media, institution, or
practice
-> transmission and mutation
-> recoverable pattern of thought development
```

## Immediate Workbench

Start from existing internal anchors:

- action-first abstraction;
- action-symbol archaeology;
- cultural drift;
- Culture as Externalized Instructions;
- Cultural Instruction Decay;
- MEAT;
- PNA open gaps.

## Do Not Do Yet

- Do not claim total reconstruction is possible.
- Do not collapse all culture into cognition.
- Do not treat all artifacts as equally informative.
- Do not infer mental states directly from artifact form.
- Do not make a linear progress ladder without counterexamples.
- Do not promote to canon before source verification and case tests.
