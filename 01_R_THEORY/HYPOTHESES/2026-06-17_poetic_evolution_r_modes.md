## Short Form

An active hypothesis mapping the user's creative output (poems, prose) to R-modes (R0 to R3) over chronological phases (2010–2024), and analyzing how emotional impotence/humility models the closing of the Decoherence Gate (DG).

---

## 1. Poetic Evolution across R-Modes

### Core Claim (HYP)
[HYP] Creative/literary output acts as an externalized trace of temporal arbitration modes (R-modes). The dominant cognitive mode of the author changes over time and is directly reflected in stylistic and structural markers of their texts:
- **2010–2012 (R0->R2)**: Raw, somatic, reactive body energy (R0) mixed with early simulation of anxiety/fear (R2).
- **2013 (R2 -> R3 emergence)**: Assembly of the core. Language itself becomes an object of structure, word construction, and ring-like self-enclosure (R3), while the narrative theme remains R2 (avoiding uncertainty, winter/cold environment).
- **2014 (R3 + R1)**: Social and political models. R1 functions as a satirical cudgel (moral rules, clear accusations), but the underlying structure is R3 (how power/norms produce subjects).
- **2015–2016 (R3 + Techno-Anthropology)**: Focus on digital alienation, the digital ghost, and the subject as an "information illusion" (R3).
- **2017–2018 (R3 + DG-heavy)**: The tone darkens. Focus shifts to impotence, decay, and the closing of the choice window (Decoherence Gate).
- **2024 (R3 Inward)**: Meta-identity where the conversation is about the subject as a tool/robot/empty shell (R3 self-model).

### Falsification Condition
Ломается, если независимый слепой анализ текстов пользователя разных лет не покажет статистически значимых изменений в структуре и лексике (например, частота использования абстрактных грамматических конструкторов, кольцевых структур, мета-высказываний и биологических метафор останется неизменной на протяжении всех периодов).

---

## 2. Impotence/Humility and the Decoherence Gate (DG)

### Core Claim (HYP)
[HYP] The emotional state of "realized impotence" (бессилие) and absolute humility (смирение) is the human subjective equivalent of Decoherence Gate (DG) closure. It is not pain or trauma that destroys personality, but the closure of the future window of choice due to exhaustion rather than resolution. This is represented by the "fish department" metaphor (decay/degeneration of the active subject).

### Falsification Condition
Ломается, если психологические исследования субъектов в состоянии глубокого бессилия/выгорания покажут, что их модель будущего (R2-симуляция) сохраняет высокий уровень разнообразия альтернативных траекторий, а не сворачивается в одну неизбежную линию угасания.
