## Source-level claims used

Nyayanga / Science 2023 is used as evidence that Oldowan technology is geographically and behaviorally broader than a single local episode and is associated with both stone-tool use and dietary processing traces.

Namorotukunan / Nature Communications 2025 is used because it reports Oldowan assemblages from roughly 2.75 to 2.44 Ma and emphasizes technological continuity / consistent technological approaches over about 300,000 years.

## R Theory use

These sources support Oldowan as a candidate for the `action-symbol threshold`:

```text
strike / detach -> sharp edge
```

The key is not merely stone use. The key is that an operation becomes a portable technological category: produce sharp edges by controlled percussion.

## Fit to action-symbol criteria

| Criterion | Source support | R Theory status |

## Interpretation

Oldowan is the first strong candidate for the moment where repeated operation becomes a portable action category.

Working status:

```text
action-symbol threshold candidate
```

This supports the architecture:

```text
proto-operation -> portable action category
```

## Use in current hypothesis

Oldowan should be treated as the lower bound for the action-symbol layer, not as proof of full symbolic thought.

It strengthens the claim that abstraction can begin as operation-stabilization rather than language or explicit symbolic meaning.

## Non-claims

Do not claim from these sources alone:

- full R3;
- language;
- symbolic meaning;
- complete action grammar;
- single-maker species certainty;
- that all Oldowan artefacts reflect the same cognitive route.
