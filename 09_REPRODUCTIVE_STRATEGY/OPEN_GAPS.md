## Postpartum Mating Strategy / Offspring Bet-Hedging

Open gaps:

1. Build a source-backed evidence map across animal strategy, cross-cultural human evidence, and modern behavioral/psychological evidence.
2. Separate these candidate mechanisms:
   - genetic diversification;
   - good genes;
   - mate switching;
   - resource acquisition;
   - paternity confusion / investment diversification;
   - postpartum fertility timing;
   - parental-care cue attractiveness;
   - observer/salience alternative.
3. Test whether observed attention from women with small children is better explained by reproductive interest, safety scanning, coalition scanning, child-mediated social openness, or observer salience.
4. Avoid treating the hypothesis as a universal claim about women or as canon before external evidence review.
