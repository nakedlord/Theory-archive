## Current Provisional Core

A religious culture can accumulate rituals and helper-figures when its central practical criterion is salvation and the mechanism of salvation is opaque. If the perceived stake is infinite and the causal channel is unclear, discarding a ritual becomes risky: a practice is preserved not because it is fully explained, but because it might save, might help, or has been reported to help someone.

This provisional core is hypothesis-level only.
